Terms

  1. Accommodation would be provided in fully secured hostel rooms in the campus of NSIT and these facilities would be separate for boys and girls.
  2. Each student, registered under the Accommodation Policy, would be provided with mattresses, bedding and pillow. It shall be noted that no toiletries or buckets would be provided.
  3. Payment for accommodation would only be accepted in cash (no online registration or card payment are accepted) and the procedure would take place at the Documentation Desk to be set up on campus.
  4. All registered students can avail the accommodation services from 9th – 13th March 2016.
  5. The check-in time for registration at the hostel desk is 12 PM and the checkout time is 10 AM on the following day. At the time of check-out, the registered students are required to return all the commodities provided to them by the organizers.
  6. The accommodation charges are INR 200 per day per participant including 4 meals per day.
  7. On reaching the NSIT campus, each registered student would be given an Accommodation Pass after the payment of the aforementioned charges. It is mandatory for all registered students to carry this pass throughout their stay at the campus.
  8. All registered students must bring their valid College Identity card and a passport size photograph, for the issuance of the Accommodation Pass.
  9. Allotment of the rooms made by the Moksha team will be final and binding.
  10. Moksha, NSIT does not bear any responsibility for any material loss during your stay at the campus. It is recommended that you carry minimum amount of valuable items.
  11. Please be cautious during the stay. The registered student is responsible and liable for any breakages or damage that is caused during their stay in the allotted room, including any accidents that may occur. If any damage is caused, the concerned student is liable to full cost of replacement or repair.
